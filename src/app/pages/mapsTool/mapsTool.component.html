MAPS Tool Interface

MAPS will be a web-hosted tool, communicating estimates of dietary micronutrient (MN) supplies and deficiency risks at national and sub-national scales in Africa.

Quick MAPS

Launch the MAPS Tool using default mode settings to explore micronutrient deficiencies in your geography of interest and possible interventions to mitigate the MNDs

My MAPS

Log-in to create or access your stored workspace, including uploading your own data

Choices in MAPS

Launch the MAPS Tool with data and model selection options enabled for you to make bespoke choices

Sharing MAPS

Log-in to a shared MAPS Tool workspace
