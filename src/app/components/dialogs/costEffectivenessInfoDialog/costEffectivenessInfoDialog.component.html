What is cost-effectiveness?

The cost-effectiveness module allows you to predict the effectiveness, cost, and cost-effectiveness of existing and hypothetical micronutrient intervention programs. You can look at interventions individually, or compare the effectiveness, cost, and cost-effectiveness of multiple interventions.
