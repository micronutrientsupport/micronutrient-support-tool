The MAPS tool's cost and effectiveness functionality is being iteratively released, to allow potential users to explore the interface and provide us with early feedback on usability.

We are initially releasing parameterised models for wheat flour and oil LSFF in Ethiopia and Malawi; however, these models are not fully functional and not all aspects can currently be used to completion, and we do not recommend data is used as it stands.

Please contact us using the ‘Submit Feedback’ button on the right side of the screen, or by email to feedback@micronutrient.support for further information. We will update this message as our functionality is finalised for use, and new features are added.

If you would like to request, or up-vote, features operationalised in the tool, please use the Feature Request Portal to do this.
